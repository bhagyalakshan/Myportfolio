About Me

Hi, I’m Bhagya Lakshan, a passionate Full Stack Developer and Tech Enthusiast dedicated to turning ideas into impactful digital experiences. With a keen interest in emerging technologies and AI-driven solutions, I strive to build innovative applications that solve real-world problems.

Over the years, I’ve combined my technical expertise with a strong commitment to community and education, mentoring students through initiatives like the Career Compass program and contributing to CSR projects that enhance access to knowledge and opportunities.

I thrive at the intersection of technology, creativity, and social impact, whether developing scalable web applications, exploring AI and Machine Learning, or volunteering to empower the next generation of innovators. When I’m not coding, I enjoy exploring new places, staying up-to-date with the latest tech trends, and sharing knowledge with others — all while continuing to grow as a technology leader and creating solutions that make a meaningful difference.
